Press F4 will let you maximize / restore the dictionary.

Press Tab will let you go to Dictionary Search Box.

Press ~ to switch between English and Vietnamese word when typing in Search Box.

In Dictionary Search Box, press Ctrl will let you find recent words that have been found before.

In Dictionary Search Box, press Down button will let you see suggestion words.

Move Mouse over word's meanings will let you see its examples.

Shift + Right Click on a word meaning will pop up a window containing the meaning and its example.
